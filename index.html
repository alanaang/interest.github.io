Gardens By The Bay


A national garden and premier horticultural attraction for local and international visitors, Gardens by the Bay is a showpiece of horticulture and garden artistry that presents the plant kingdom in a whole new way, entertaining while educating visitors with plants seldom seen in this part of the world, ranging from species in cool, temperate climates to tropical forests and habitats.

Attractions

Flower Dome

Step into the Flower Dome and stand in awe of nature. Spectacular and innovative, it is the largest glass greenhouse in the world as listed in the 2015 Guinness World Records!

Be amazed by changing display of flowers and plants from the Mediterranean and semi-arid regions.

Cloud Forest

Enter the Cloud Forest, a mysterious world veiled in mist. Take in breath-taking mountain views surrounded by diverse vegetation and hidden floral gems.

And learn about rare plants and their fast-disappearing environment.


Hours & Admissions
  • Flower Dome & Cloud Forest, Open daily: 9.00am - 9.00am
  • Recommended duration for visiting One Conservatory (Flower Dome): 1 hour
  • Recommended duration for visiting Two Conservatories (Flower Dome & Cloud Forest): 2 hours
  • Cloud Forest Mist Timings: 10.00am, 12.00pm, 2.00pm, 4.00pm, 6.00pm, 8.00pm daily

Bay East Garden

Step into a picturesque world where beautiful pavilions, lush wide-open lawns and elegant palm trees await.

With glorious views of the Marina Bay skyline, Bay East Garden offers scenic tranquility in the city.

Supertree Grove

Measuring between 25 and 50 metres tall, Gardens by the Bay's Supertrees are designed with large canopies that provide shade in the day and come alive with an exhilarating display of lights and sounds at night. Stand in awe amidst these iconic giants, stroll along the 22-metre-high OCBC Skyway and enjoy panoramic vistas of the Gardens and the Marina Bay skyline at our new Supertree Observatory.


Hours & Admissions
  • Supertree Grove, Open daily: 5.00am - 2.00am
  • Garden Rhapsody Show at 7.45pm and 8.45pm
  • Supertree Observatory & OCBC Skyway, Open daily: 9.00am - 9.00pm
  • Admission to Supertree Grove is free but admission charge to OCBC Skyway and Supertree Observatory apply.
